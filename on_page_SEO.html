What is On-Page SEO?

On-page SEO refers to the practices used to optimize individual web pages to rank higher and earn more relevant traffic in search engines. This includes optimizing the content, HTML source code, and website structure.

Best Practices for On-Page SEO

1. Use Relevant Keywords

Incorporate keywords naturally into your content, headings, and meta tags. For example, "on-page SEO" is a primary keyword used on this page.

2. Optimize Title and Meta Description

Your title and meta description should be engaging and include your primary keywords.

3. Mobile-Friendly Design

Ensure your website is responsive and provides a great user experience on all devices.

4. Add Internal Links

Link to other relevant pages on your website to help users navigate and improve site structure.

5. Use Alt Text for Images

Describe your images with alt text to improve accessibility and help search engines understand their content.

Conclusion

By following these on-page SEO best practices, you can improve your website's visibility and attract more organic traffic. Start optimizing your pages today!
